How we Work

Algorithm for working with us for convenience and understanding of the project

  • Idea

    The client comes to us with an idea – this could be an image, a sketch, or any other reference. We analyze how to bring it to life, create technical drawings, and suggest suitable materials.

  • Technical Specification

    We turn the idea into a detailed technical task. This includes drawings and preliminary engineering solutions based on the client’s vision.

  • Commercial Offer

    We prefer to work with natural materials, but we can adapt any budget by simplifying materials or design. We align the commercial offer with the client’s expectations and financial limits.

  • Preparation of a working project

    We prepare all working documentation and technical drawings – this is the foundation for high-quality production. At this stage, we finalize all dimensions, materials, and designs with the client before production begins.

  • Production & Installation

    Production takes from 15 working days, depending on complexity and volume. We also handle installation ourselves – quickly and with precision.
